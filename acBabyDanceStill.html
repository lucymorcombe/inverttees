Donation

With every purchase you make at Invert Tees, you get to select a charity who will receive 33.33% of the cost of your purchase. You can read more about each charity on our charities page.

akt formerly Albert Kennedy Trust

akt has been providing routes to safe housing, support, and secure futures for LGBTQ+ young people since 1989. They are a UK-wide charity with centres in four major cities.

Black Minds Matter UK

Black Minds Matter UK is a fully registered charity connecting Black individuals and families with free therapy by qualified and accredited Black therapists.

Galop

Galop is the UK’s LGBT+ anti-abuse charity. We work with and for LGBT+ victims and survivors of abuse and violence.

Mermaids

Mermaids supports transgender, nonbinary and gender-diverse children and young people until their 20th birthday, as well as their families and professionals involved in their care.

MindOut

MindOut is a mental health service run by and for lesbian, gay, bisexual, trans, and queer (LGBTQ) people.

Palestine Children's Relief Fund

Palestine Children's Relief Fund (PCRF), founded in 1991 by concerned humanitarians in the USA, provides free medical care to thousands of injured and ill children yearly who lack local access to care within the local health care system.

Refuge

Refuge is the largest specialist domestic abuse organisation in the UK. On any given day our services support thousands of survivors, helping them to overcome the physical, emotional, financial and logistical impacts of abuse and rebuild their lives — free from fear.

Terrence Higgins Trust

Terrence Higgins Trust is the UK’s leading HIV and sexual health charity, and one of the largest in Europe.
