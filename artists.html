Hannah Norberg-Williams

Hanna is an award-winning visual creative based in Cardiff. They create distinctive bold, textural work in illustration, animation and graphic design that has been featured in various publications and exhibitions. Their work combines juxtaposed colours and scenarios that often subvert the everyday with the surreal and absurd.
